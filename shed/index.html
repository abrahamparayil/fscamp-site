Schedule

September 19-October 15 Mentors & Learners Application Window People who wish to join the camp as mentor or learner can apply during this time.
October 17-October 24 Ice Breaking Learners get to know each other and know more about the program
October 25-November 3 Introduction to Free Software Get to know free software philosophy and install a GNU/Linux OS
November 4-November 11 Explore different areas of contribution Learn about different free software projects from contributors, and meet mentors. Exploration of potential ideas to work on in the later phase.
November 12-November 19 Choose a way forward Learners finalize one/two proposals for working on free software project(s) with mentors. Proposals should be submitted before 23:59 on November 19.
November 20-November 22 Mentor assignment Proposals are evaluated and learners are assigned mentors.
November 23 Start working with mentors Learners can begin work as per their proposals
December 19 Last date for first report Learners submit an interim report with their progress.
December 20 First review meeting Camp regroups for evaluation and review of progress.
January 22 Submit second report Learners submit an interim report with their progress.
January 23 Second review meeting Camp regroups for evaluation and review of progress.
February 20 Submit final report Learners submit a final report about their project.
February 27 Final review meeting Camp regroups for final review of their project. Closing ceremony
February 28 The After-party The camp never ends. You can continue your free software contributions and we will always remain a family.
